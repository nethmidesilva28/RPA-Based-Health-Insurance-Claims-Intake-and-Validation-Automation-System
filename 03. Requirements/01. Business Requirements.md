# Business Requirements

This document outlines the high-level business needs for automating the Health Insurance Claims Intake and Validation process using RPA.

## BR-01: Automated Claim Intake
The system must automatically capture incoming health insurance claims from email, web forms, or uploaded documents without manual intervention.

## BR-02: Policy Validation
The system must validate whether the submitted policy number exists and is active during the incident date.

## BR-03: Document Verification
The system must ensure that all mandatory supporting documents are present based on claim type.

## BR-04: Claim Classification
The system must classify claims into:
- Valid Claims
- Incomplete Claims
- Exception Cases

## BR-05: Automated Routing
The system must route claims to appropriate queues based on validation results.

## BR-06: Exception Handling
The system must identify invalid or incomplete claims and route them for manual review.

## BR-07: Notifications
The system must send automated notifications to claimants for missing information or claim status updates.

## BR-08: Audit Logging
All actions performed by the automation system must be logged for traceability and compliance purposes.
