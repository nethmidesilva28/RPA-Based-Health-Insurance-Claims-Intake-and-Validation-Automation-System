# Functional Requirements

This section defines what the system must do to meet business requirements.

## FR-01: Claim Data Capture
The system shall extract claim details from emails, PDFs, or online submissions.

## FR-02: Data Extraction
The system shall extract key fields such as:
- Policy number
- Claimant (Policy Holder) name
- Incident date (Date of Service)
- Claim type
- Supporting documents

## FR-03: Policy Validation
The system shall verify policy number existence and active status from the policy database.

## FR-04: Document Validation
The system shall check for mandatory documents based on claim type.

## FR-05: Business Rule Execution
The system shall apply predefined business rules to determine claim validity.

## FR-06: Claim Classification
The system shall categorize claims into valid, incomplete, or exception categories.

## FR-07: Routing Engine
The system shall route claims to appropriate queues based on classification.

## FR-08: Notification Engine
The system shall send automated emails for:
- Claim confirmation
- Missing documents
- Exception alerts

## FR-09: Logging
The system shall log all transactions and decisions made during processing.
