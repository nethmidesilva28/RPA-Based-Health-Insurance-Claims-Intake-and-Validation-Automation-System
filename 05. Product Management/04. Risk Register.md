# Risk Register

This document outlines potential risks associated with implementing the RPA solution.

---

## R1: Inaccurate Data Extraction
- **Description:** OCR or extraction may fail for poor-quality documents
- **Impact:** Incorrect claim data processing
- **Mitigation:** Use validation rules and manual review for uncertain cases

---

## R2: System Integration Failures
- **Description:** Policy or claims system may not support stable integration
- **Impact:** Automation breakdown or delays
- **Mitigation:** Use fallback UI automation and error handling

---

## R3: Changes in Business Rules
- **Description:** Insurance policies and rules may change frequently
- **Impact:** Bot logic becomes outdated
- **Mitigation:** Maintain configurable rule engine

---

## R4: High Exception Volume
- **Description:** Many claims may fall into exception category
- **Impact:** Reduced automation efficiency
- **Mitigation:** Improve validation logic iteratively

---

## R5: Data Security Risks
- **Description:** Sensitive patient data handled by automation
- **Impact:** Compliance and privacy issues
- **Mitigation:** Apply encryption, access control, and audit logs

---

## R6: System Downtime
- **Description:** Dependency systems may be unavailable
- **Impact:** Processing delays
- **Mitigation:** Queue-based retry mechanism and fallback processing
