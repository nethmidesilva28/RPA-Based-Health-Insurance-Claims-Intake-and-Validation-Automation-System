# Test Cases

This document defines sample test cases for validating the RPA-based Health Insurance Claims Intake and Validation solution.

---

## TC-01: Process a Valid Claim

**Test Case ID:** TC-01  
**Test Scenario:** Valid claim submission with all required details and documents  
**Precondition:** Claim includes valid policy number, valid incident date, and all mandatory documents  

### Steps:
1. Submit a health insurance claim with complete details
2. Trigger bot processing
3. Validate policy number against policy system
4. Check document completeness
5. Review classification result

### Expected Result:
- Claim is marked as **Valid**
- Claim is routed to the **Valid Claims Queue**
- Processing log is created

---

## TC-02: Identify Missing Mandatory Document

**Test Case ID:** TC-02  
**Test Scenario:** Claim submitted without mandatory hospital invoice  
**Precondition:** Claim contains valid policy details but one required document is missing  

### Steps:
1. Submit claim without hospital invoice
2. Trigger bot processing
3. Validate policy details
4. Run document completeness check

### Expected Result:
- Missing document is detected
- Claim is marked as **Incomplete**
- Claim is routed to the **Incomplete Claims Queue**
- Missing document notification is triggered

---

## TC-03: Detect Invalid Policy Number

**Test Case ID:** TC-03  
**Test Scenario:** Claim submitted with policy number not found in the system  
**Precondition:** Claim is submitted with an invalid/non-existent policy number  

### Steps:
1. Submit claim with invalid policy number
2. Trigger bot processing
3. Validate policy in policy system

### Expected Result:
- Policy validation fails
- Claim is marked as **Exception**
- Claim is routed to the **Exception Review Queue**
- Error is logged

---

## TC-04: Detect Expired Policy

**Test Case ID:** TC-04  
**Test Scenario:** Claim submitted under an expired policy  
**Precondition:** Claim has a policy number that exists but is inactive on the incident date  

### Steps:
1. Submit claim with expired policy
2. Trigger bot processing
3. Validate policy status and incident date

### Expected Result:
- Policy is identified as inactive/expired
- Claim is marked as **Exception**
- Claim is routed to the **Exception Review Queue**

## TC-05: Flag Duplicate Claim

**Test Case ID:** TC-05  
**Test Scenario:** Same claim submitted twice  
**Precondition:** A claim with matching policy number, incident date, and claim type already exists  

### Steps:
1. Submit duplicate claim
2. Trigger bot processing
3. Execute duplicate claim validation rule

### Expected Result:
- Duplicate is detected
- Claim is marked for manual review
- Claim is routed to the **Exception Review Queue**
- Duplicate reason is logged

## TC-06: Handle Corrupted Attachment

**Test Case ID:** TC-06  
**Test Scenario:** Claim includes unreadable supporting document  
**Precondition:** Submitted attachment is corrupted or unreadable by bot/OCR  

### Steps:
