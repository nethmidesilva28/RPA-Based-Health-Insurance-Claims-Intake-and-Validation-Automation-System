# Bot Workflow Logic

This document describes the step-by-step workflow of the RPA bot.

## Step 1: Trigger Event
- Bot is triggered when a new claim is received via:
  - Email inbox
  - Claims submission portal
  - Uploaded batch files

## Step 2: Claim Data Extraction
- Bot extracts:
  - Policy number
  - Claimant name
  - Claim type
  - Incident date
  - Supporting documents

## Step 3: Data Structuring
- Extracted data is converted into structured format
- Data is stored temporarily for validation processing

## Step 4: Policy Validation
- Bot checks policy number in policy database
- Confirms if policy is active on incident date

## Step 5: Document Check
- Bot verifies presence of required documents based on claim type

## Step 6: Business Rule Execution
- Bot applies predefined validation rules to determine claim status

## Step 7: Claim Classification
- Claim is classified as:
  - Valid
  - Incomplete
  - Exception

## Step 8: Routing
- Bot sends claim to appropriate queue based on classification

## Step 9: Logging
- All actions are logged for audit and monitoring purposes
