# Automation Overview

This section describes the RPA-based solution designed to automate the Health Insurance Claims Intake and Validation process.

## Objective of Automation
The primary goal of the automation is to reduce manual effort in claims intake and validation by automating repetitive, rule-based tasks such as:
- Claim data extraction
- Policy validation
- Document completeness checks
- Claim classification
- Routing to appropriate queues

## High-Level Solution Concept
The RPA bot acts as a digital worker that:
1. Monitors incoming claim submissions (email/forms/system uploads)
2. Extracts structured data from unstructured inputs
3. Validates claims using predefined business rules
4. Classifies claims based on validation results
5. Routes claims to appropriate processing queues
6. Logs all actions for audit and compliance

## Key Benefits
- Faster claim processing at intake stage
- Reduced manual workload for claims officers
- Improved consistency in validation
- Reduced human errors in data entry and routing
