# Health Insurance Claims Intake & Validation Automation (RPA Case Study)

## Project Overview

This repository contains a **Business Analysis and Product Management case study** focused on designing an **RPA-based Health Insurance Claims Intake & Validation Automation solution**. The case study explores how repetitive and rule-based tasks in the health insurance claims process can be streamlined through automation to improve efficiency, reduce manual effort, and enhance validation accuracy.

The project covers the end-to-end analysis and design of an automation solution for the **claims intake stage**, including:

* current-state process analysis
* pain point identification
* business and functional requirements
* RPA workflow design
* validation and routing logic
* testing scenarios and documentation
* final case study report

## Business Problem

Health insurance claims intake often involves manual review of claim submissions, policy verification, document completeness checks, and routing of claims for further processing. These tasks are repetitive, time-consuming, and prone to delays or human error, especially when claim volumes are high.

This case study proposes an **RPA-driven solution** to automate the early-stage claims workflow by validating incoming claims, identifying incomplete submissions, and routing claims to the correct processing queues.

## Project Scope

The automation scope focuses on:

* claim intake from digital channels
* policy validation
* mandatory document checks
* claim classification
* exception handling
* queue-based routing
