# Success Metrics

The success of the RPA-based claims intake and validation system will be measured using the following KPIs:

## Operational Efficiency:
- Reduction in average claim intake processing time by 60–80%
- Increase in number of claims processed per day without additional staff

## Accuracy & Quality:
- Improvement in validation accuracy to above 95%
- Reduction in manual data entry errors by at least 70%

## Process Improvement:
- Reduction in incomplete or incorrectly routed claims
- Decrease in backlog at the claims intake stage by 40–60%

## Automation Performance:
- Percentage of claims successfully processed by RPA without human intervention (target: 70–85%)
- Reduction in repetitive manual tasks for claims officers

## Customer Impact:
- Faster acknowledgement of submitted claims
- Reduced turnaround time for claim status updates
