# Problem Statement

The current health insurance claims intake and validation process is heavily manual and inefficient. Claims officers must manually review incoming claims, validate policy information, check supporting documents, and determine claim completeness before routing them for further processing.

This leads to:
- Long processing times for claim intake
- Inconsistent validation due to human dependency
- High administrative workload for claims officers
- Delays in routing claims to the correct teams
- Increased risk of missing or incorrect data entry

There is a need for an automated, rule-based solution that can handle repetitive validation and routing tasks while ensuring accuracy, consistency, and improved operational efficiency.
