# Assumptions and Constraints

## Assumptions:
- Claims are submitted in a structured or semi-structured format (email, PDF, or form)
- Policy data is available in a centralized internal system
- Required document rules are predefined by the insurance provider
- RPA bots have access to necessary systems (claims database, policy database)
- Business rules for validation are clearly defined and stable during implementation

## Constraints:
- The RPA system is limited to rule-based decision-making and cannot perform complex medical or fraud analysis
- Some claims may still require human intervention due to missing or unclear data
- System integration depends on availability of APIs or system access permissions
- Changes in policy rules or claim formats may require updates to bot logic
- OCR accuracy may vary depending on document quality
