# Project Objectives

The primary objective of this project is to design an RPA-based solution that automates the health insurance claims intake and validation process.

## Specific Objectives:
- Automate the intake of health insurance claims from multiple channels (email/forms)
- Validate policy information against internal systems
- Check completeness of required claim documents
- Identify incomplete or invalid claims early in the process
- Classify and route claims to appropriate processing queues
- Reduce manual effort involved in initial claim handling
- Improve consistency and accuracy in claim validation
- Reduce overall claims processing time at the intake stage
