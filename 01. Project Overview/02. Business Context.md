# Business Context

Health insurance providers process a high volume of claims submitted by policyholders, hospitals, and healthcare providers. These claims typically include medical invoices, policy details, supporting documents, and incident information.

In the current environment, claims intake and validation is largely manual. Claims officers are responsible for:
- Reviewing submitted claim forms
- Verifying policy details in internal systems
- Checking required supporting documents
- Identifying incomplete or invalid claims
- Routing claims to appropriate processing teams

This manual process is time-consuming and prone to human error, especially during peak claim periods.

As the volume of claims increases, insurers face challenges in maintaining efficiency, reducing turnaround time, and ensuring consistent validation standards. This creates a strong opportunity for RPA-driven automation in the claims intake and validation stage.
