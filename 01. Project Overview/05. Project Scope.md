# Project Scope

## In Scope:
- Intake of health insurance claims from email or digital submission sources
- Extraction of key claim details (policy number, claimant details, claim type, etc.)
- Validation of policy existence and status
- Verification of mandatory supporting documents
- Identification of incomplete or invalid claims
- Classification of claims based on type and completeness
- Routing of claims to appropriate queues (valid, incomplete, exception)
- Notification of missing information to claimants or internal teams

## Out of Scope:
- Final claim approval or rejection decisions
- Fraud detection or advanced analytics
- Claim payment processing or reimbursement
- Medical assessment or clinical validation
- Integration with external hospital systems beyond basic data validation
