# Project Summary

This project focuses on designing an RPA-based Health Insurance Claims Intake and Validation Automation system.

The goal is to streamline the early-stage claims process by automating repetitive and rule-based tasks such as claim intake, policy validation, document completeness checks, and routing of claims to appropriate queues.

Currently, claims officers manually handle these tasks, leading to delays, inconsistencies, and high operational workload. The proposed solution introduces a rule-based automation workflow that reduces manual effort and improves processing accuracy.

This case study demonstrates a Business Analysis and Product Management approach to identifying process inefficiencies, defining business requirements, and designing an automation-ready future-state workflow.
