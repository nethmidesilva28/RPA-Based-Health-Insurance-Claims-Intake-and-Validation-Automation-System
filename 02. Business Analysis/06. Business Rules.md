# Business Rules

The following rules govern the validation and processing of health insurance claims.

## 1. Policy Validation Rules
- Policy number must exist in the system
- Policy must be active on the date of incident
- Claim type must be covered under the policy

## 2. Claim Completeness Rules
- Claim form must be submitted
- Patient/claimant details must be present
- Incident date must be provided
- Mandatory supporting documents must be attached

## 3. Document Requirements
- Medical claims require hospital invoice and discharge summary
- Emergency claims may require additional proof (e.g., admission records)
- Missing documents must be flagged as incomplete claims

## 4. Duplicate Claim Rules
- Claims with same policy number, date, and incident type must be flagged

## 5. Data Validation Rules
- All mandatory fields must not be empty
- Dates must follow valid format and logic (incident date cannot be in future)

## 6. Routing Rules
- Valid claims → Claims Assessment Queue
- Incomplete claims → Document Pending Queue
- Invalid policy claims → Exception Review Queue
