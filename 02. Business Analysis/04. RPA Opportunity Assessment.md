# RPA Opportunity Assessment

The claims intake and validation process contains multiple tasks suitable for RPA automation.

## High Automation Potential Tasks:

### 1. Claim Data Extraction
- Extract data from emails, PDFs, and forms
- Structured and repetitive task

### 2. Policy Verification
- Check policy number validity in internal system
- Rule-based lookup process

### 3. Document Validation
- Verify presence of mandatory documents
- Checklist-based validation

### 4. Claim Classification
- Categorize claims based on predefined rules
- Decision-tree logic suitable for bots

### 5. Routing Logic
- Forward claims to correct queues based on status
- Fully rule-based workflow

### 6. Notifications
- Send automated emails for missing or invalid claims
- Template-based communication

## Medium Automation Potential:
- Exception handling (partial automation with human review)
- Data reconciliation between systems

## Low Automation Potential:
- Medical assessment decisions
- Fraud detection and investigation
- Complex case resolution requiring human judgment
