# Exception Scenarios

This section outlines scenarios where claims cannot be fully processed by automation and require human intervention.

## 1. Missing Mandatory Documents
- Required medical reports or invoices not attached
- Bot flags claim as incomplete

## 2. Invalid Policy Number
- Policy not found in system
- Claim sent to exception queue

## 3. Expired Policy
- Policy not active on incident date
- Requires manual verification

## 4. Unclear or Corrupted Documents
- OCR cannot read uploaded files
- Manual review required

## 5. Duplicate Claim Submission
- Same claim submitted multiple times
- Flagged for review to prevent duplication

## 6. Mismatched Claim Details
- Claim type does not match policy coverage
- Routed for manual assessment

## 7. System Unavailability
- Policy or claims system is down during validation
- Claim queued for retry processing

## 8. Edge Case Scenarios
- Special cases not covered by existing business rules
- Requires manual decision-making
