# Stakeholder Analysis

This section identifies the key stakeholders involved in the Health Insurance Claims Intake and Validation process.

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. Policyholders (Claimants)
- Submit health insurance claims
- Expect fast processing and accurate updates
- Provide supporting documents and claim details

### 2. Claims Intake Officers
- Manually review incoming claims
- Validate policy details and documents
- Enter claim data into internal systems
- Route claims to appropriate departments

### 3. Claims Assessors
- Handle validated claims for further assessment
- Review medical and financial eligibility
- Make approval/rejection recommendations

## Secondary Stakeholders

### 4. Operations Manager
- Oversees claims processing efficiency
- Monitors KPIs such as turnaround time and backlog
- Ensures SLA compliance

### 5. Compliance / Risk Team
- Ensures claims handling follows regulatory and internal policies
- Reviews audit logs and exception cases

### 6. IT / RPA Development Team
- Designs, builds, and maintains automation bots
- Ensures system integration and bot stability

### 7. Customer Support Team
- Handles inquiries related to claim status
- Communicates missing information or delays to claimants
