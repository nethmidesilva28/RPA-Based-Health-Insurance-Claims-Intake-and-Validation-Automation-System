# Pain Point Analysis

The current manual claims intake process presents several operational challenges:

## 1. High Manual Effort
- Claims officers spend significant time on repetitive tasks such as data entry and document checking

## 2. Processing Delays
- Manual validation slows down the intake process
- Claims backlog increases during peak periods

## 3. Inconsistent Validation
- Different officers may interpret rules differently
- Leads to inconsistencies in claim approval readiness

## 4. Data Entry Errors
- Manual input increases the risk of incorrect or missing information in the system

## 5. Poor Visibility
- Limited tracking of claim status at intake stage
- Difficult to identify bottlenecks in real time

## 6. Inefficient Routing
- Claims are sometimes routed to incorrect queues, causing rework and delays

## 7. High Operational Cost
- More human effort is required to manage increasing claim volumes
